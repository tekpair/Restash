Bulk Seller Agreement

Effective June 23, 2026 · Restash

This Bulk Seller Agreement (the "Agreement") governs participation in the Restash Bulk Seller Program (the "Program"), a lane for high-volume sellers. It supplements our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy; where they differ for Bulk Sellers, this Agreement controls. By applying to or participating in the Program, you agree to it.

1. Eligibility & application

To apply, your Restash account must have at least 50 paid claims, be at least 60 days old, and be in good standing (no counterfeit or repeated condition-mismatch flags). You must also be able to provide a valid government ID for verification and accept this Agreement. You apply from the Bulk Seller section of your account. Meeting the requirements does not guarantee acceptance — membership is granted at Restash's sole discretion.

2. Approval & status

After you apply, your application is under review until the Restash team approves or declines it; we email you the decision. Once approved, your membership is active. Restash may suspend or close a membership as described in this Agreement. Your current status is always shown in your account.

3. How bulk selling works

  1. You submit one manifest describing the whole lot.
  2. We email you a prepaid shipping label — there is no per-claim review gate.
  3. You ship the lot using our label.
  4. On arrival the lot receives priority inspection. Bulk lots are inspected as a whole and skip the standard one-by-one review queue.
  5. We make a single offer for the entire lot.
  6. You accept or decline the offer in your account. If you accept, we authorize payment; if you decline, we return the lot and email tracking.

4. Ongoing requirements

While your membership is active you must:

  • Ship at least 50 games per month.
  • Send a minimum of 15 games per shipment.

5. Suspension & closure

If you do not reach the monthly minimum, your membership is suspended. To be reinstated, ship 50 games within the suspension month. If you do not, your Bulk Seller membership is closed, and you may reapply after 3 months. Restash may also suspend or close a membership immediately for any violation of this Agreement.

6. Authenticity, condition & conduct

You may submit only authentic, working games that you lawfully own and have the right to sell. Counterfeit, reproduction, pirated, or non-working copies, ownership issues, and repeated condition mismatches are not permitted and may result in immediate closure of your membership, in addition to the consequences described in the Terms of Service (including for suspected stolen property).

7. Account security & access

Your membership is personal to you (or your business). Sharing or selling access to your account — or letting anyone else sell through it — is not allowed and may result in immediate closure. Keep your credentials secure.

8. Exclusive lane

While your membership is active, you sell only through the Bulk Seller lane; the standard one-by-one flow is unavailable on your account. If your membership ends (declined, closed, or you leave), you may use Restash normally.

9. Payments

Payment is made per accepted offer via PayPal only — the single authorized payout method for Bulk Sellers (no checks) — on the same terms as our Terms of Service. We pay out only when the name on the PayPal account matches the name on your Restash account. Restash does not hold a withdrawable balance.

10. Direct support

Active Bulk Sellers get a 24/7 direct line to our team to coordinate manifests, shipments, and offers.

11. Changes & leaving the Program

We may update this Agreement and will post the updated version with a new effective date; continued participation means you accept it. You may leave the Program at any time by contacting us — afterward you can keep using Restash normally.

12. Contact

Questions about the Program? Email hello@getrestash.gg.
