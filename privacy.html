Privacy Policy

Effective June 16, 2026 · Restash

This Privacy Policy explains what information Restash collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using Restash you agree to this policy.

This is a working template prepared for Restash and is not legal advice. Have it reviewed by a licensed attorney before you launch.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email, phone number, and password.
  • Claim & game details — the games you submit, their edition, condition, and quantity, and notes.
  • Payout information — your PayPal email or mailing address.
  • Technical information — including IP addresses, device and usage data.
  • Communications — emails and messages between you and Restash.

2. How we use your information

To create and secure your account; review and process claims; communicate decisions, offers, and tracking; verify your identity; issue payment; and detect and prevent fraud, counterfeits, or abuse of the platform.

3. Accounts are required

A Restash account is required to participate. Your claims, offers, and payout history are tied to your account and visible to you in My Claims.

4. Identity & payout matching

To reduce fraud, we pay out only when the name on your PayPal account or check matches the name on your Restash account. Verifying this may involve reviewing the information you provide.

5. Communications

We email you about your account and claims — confirmations, review decisions, shipping labels, arrival notices, offers, payment authorizations, and tracking. These are service communications related to your transactions.

6. Fraud prevention & law enforcement

If we suspect unlawful activity, such as the sale of stolen or counterfeit games, we may retain relevant information and share it with law enforcement, and take action on your account as described in our Terms of Service.
