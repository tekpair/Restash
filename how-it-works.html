How it works

Turn your games into cash in a few steps.

Build a claim, ship your games to us free, and get paid by PayPal or check once you accept our offer. No listings, no buyers to deal with.

1

Build your claim

Add your games, set the edition and condition of each, and choose how you'd like to be paid. You get an estimate as you go.

2

We review it

Usually within 2–3 business days. We accept a limited number of games and personally choose which to bring in — you get the decision in your account and by email.

3

Ship it free

If we accept your claim, we email you a prepaid shipping label. Pack your games, drop them off, and we'll confirm when they arrive.

4

We inspect & make an offer

We confirm condition and authenticity, then send you a final offer in My Claims. The offer is based on what the games actually are.

5

You accept or decline

Review the offer in your account. Accept it to get paid, or decline and we send your games back to you free.

6

Get paid

Once you accept, we pay you by PayPal (1–3 business days) or check (3–5 business days), to a name matching your account.

Frequently asked questions

The short answers. Anything else, email hello@getrestash.gg.

How much will I get for my games?
Your estimate is based on a game's current market value, adjusted for its condition — sealed, complete, and loose are priced differently. The estimate is preliminary; we confirm a final offer after we inspect the games, and you review it before any payment.
What games do you buy?
Right now we buy select PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch titles in good condition. We accept a limited number of games and choose which to bring in, so not every title or claim is accepted.
Is shipping free?
Yes. If we accept your claim, we email you a prepaid shipping label. If you decline an offer, or we decline the games, we return them to you free with tracking.
How do I get paid?
By PayPal (typically 1–3 business days) or check (typically 3–5 business days). We pay out only to a PayPal account or check in the same name as your Restash account.
How long does the whole process take?
We review your claim within about 2–3 business days. After your games arrive, we send an outcome within about 2–3 more. Once you accept an offer, payment follows by your chosen method.
What if my game is damaged or not working?
Condition affects the offer. If a game's condition turns out different from the grade you selected, we re-grade it on inspection, adjust the offer, and explain why on your claim. We don't accept counterfeit or non-working copies. If something can't be accepted, we return it to you.
Do I need an account?
Yes. A free Restash account is required to submit a claim and to be paid, and you must be at least 18 years old.
Can I sell more than one game at once?
Yes. Add multiple games to a single claim and set the quantity of each. Your estimate updates as you build the claim.
Can I cancel or decline?
Yes. You review every offer before any payment is made. Decline and we send your games back to you free.
